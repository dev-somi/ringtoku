# 📞 RingToku: 10-Minute Daily AI English Call

**RingToku** is an AI-driven English conversation application designed to lower the psychological barrier to language learning. Unlike traditional apps that wait for the user to open them, **RingToku calls you first**, ensuring a consistent 10-minute daily practice.

## 🌟 Core Concept: "The Habit-Making Call"

Many learners struggle with the "friction" of starting a study session. RingToku removes this friction by initiating the conversation through an automated AI call, making English practice as natural as answering a phone call from a friend.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Proactive AI Calling**: Receive a daily call at your scheduled time—no more "forgetting" to practice.
* **10-Minute Micro-learning**: Perfectly timed sessions to improve speaking fluency without feeling overwhelmed.
* **Natural Conversation AI**: Engaging, context-aware AI personas tailored for Japanese learners.
* **Progress Tracking**: Review your daily conversation highlights and feedback on pronunciation or expressions.

## 🛠 Tech Stack (Planned)

* **Frontend**: Flutter (Cross-platform for iOS & Android)
* **Backend**: Python / FastAPI
* **AI Engine**: LLM-based Voice AI (OpenAI GPT-4o-realtime or similar)
* **Communication**: WebRTC / VoIP Integration

## 🎯 Target Audience

* Learners who find it difficult to start studying on their own.
* Busy professionals needing a quick, high-impact speaking habit.
* Users looking for a low-pressure environment to practice English speaking.
